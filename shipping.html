Shipping Policy

Our Logistical Devotion & Commitments

We carefully pack every sacred item with absolute clean hands and respect from our centralized distribution hub in Canada. We seek to ensure your devotional tools reach your home safely and swiftly.

Processing Timelines:

All standard orders are fully processed and packed within 2 to 4 business days. Please note that order processing does not occur on Sundays or major Vaishnava holy days (such as Sri Krishna Janmashtami, Radhashtami, and Gaura Purnima) to allow our dedicated team to fully immerse themselves in festival services.

Shipping Estimated Timelines & Domestic Rates:

  • Standard Canadian Domestic Shipping: 3–7 business days (Calculated dynamically at checkout based on live carrier rates).
  • Expedited North American Shipping: 2–4 business days via Canada Post / USPS.
  • International Shipping: We ship worldwide to support the global ISKCON sangha. Delivery timelines vary between 10–21 business days based on localized customs clearance procedures.

Customs, Tariffs, and Duties Tracking:

Shreedha Artistry is not responsible for any additional customs duties, taxes, or localized tariffs applied directly to your parcel during international shipping. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the sole responsibility of the customer.
