Refund & Return Policy

Our Sacred Satisfaction Guarantee

We carefully select every item with love and gratitude. If your item arrives structurally defective or damaged during transit, we are fully committed to correcting the issue immediately.

Return Standard Windows:

We offer a transparent 30-day return policy for all unworn apparel (Gopi dresses, kurtas) and completely unused devotional accessories. To be eligible for a full return, your item must be in the exact pristine condition that you received it, completely unworn, unwashed, and neatly enclosed within its original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items due to Spiritual Purity Standards:

To preserve the absolute ritual purity (Vishauddhata) of sacred tools, we cannot under any circumstances accept returns on used Japa Malas, open chanting counters, or sacred items that have already been placed directly onto a home altar or offered to Deities.

How to Initiate a Safe Return:

Please contact our support team at support@shreedhaartistry.com with your original order number and precise photographic evidence of any shipping damages. Once verified, we will issue a prepaid return label and process your refund or replacement immediately.
